# A mental checklist during meetings

When someone says:

> "I don't think we should do it that way."

Run this silently:

1. **What problem are they optimizing for?**
2. **Which assumption differs from mine?**
3. **What evidence would change my opinion?**
4. **What constraint might I be missing?**
5. **Can I explain their view fairly before responding?**
6. **Do I want to be right, or do I want the best decision?**

| Question                                    | What it uncovers                   |
| ------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **What concern are you trying to address?** | Their goal                         |
| **Which assumption do you disagree with?**  | Their reasoning                    |
| **What risks do you see?**                  | Their experience and mental model  |
| **When would my approach be better?**       | Shared understanding of trade-offs |
| **What evidence would decide this?**        | An objective path forward          |
